Is your college Go club interested in competing in the next season of the Collegiate Go League?

It's easy! Your team captain should contact us on behalf of your school's go club, with the following information:

  • The names and ranks of players on your roster. KGS ranks are preferred. You can have as many players on your roster as you like, but only 5 people can represent your school in any given match. Each team member should be an active member of that school's Go club.
  • A team captain, responsible for managing the team. The team captain will be our contact to your club, will keep the school's roster up-to-date, and will coordinate players' login and participation during matches.
  • Other club information: Club president, club officer mailing list / contact email, website.

We will then add your club to the website, and send you more details on how to participate.

If you would like to be listed in our database without participating in league play, that's fine too - just leave out the roster.


We're a fast-growing group, so let us know how we can improve the site, answer your questions, or serve the Go community.
