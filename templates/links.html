    New to Go?

    Go is an ancient two-player board game based on simple rules that lead to immense strategic and aesthetic complexity. Also known as weiqi (China), baduk (Korea), or igo (Japan), Go combines the tactical intensity of chess with abstract, global judgment to make an exceptionally challenging and rewarding game. To learn how to play, take a look at the basics, check out our links below, or contact us with your questions.
