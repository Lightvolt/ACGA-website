The American Collegiate Go Association is a national network of schools and students dedicated to strengthening the American Go community.

Our current project is the Collegiate Go League, a new online tournament format for college Go clubs anywhere in the world. We hope that by encouraging friendly competition between clubs, we can foster a sense of Go community and help address the unique challenges and advantages for college go clubs.

Learn more about tournaments here. You can sign your school up for the next matches here - schools compete every other week, so you can get started anytime!
