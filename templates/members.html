Joining the American Collegiate Go Association


The ACGA is based in cooperation and networking between member clubs nationwide and is poised to make a big impact on Go in the United States. Many American Go players are unaware of nearby community and school-based clubs, and many clubs are unaware of the availability of local Go players. The American Collegiate Go Association intends to correct this knowledge gap and coordinate a broader university Go culture in the United States. So why not join the growing collegiate go community? It's free!

Start a New Club


Through our support with the Ing Foundation, the ACGA can provide resources and support to help you start a club at your school. It only takes one person to set up a club, and if our track record is any indication, you'll probably find dozens of other Go players on campus.
