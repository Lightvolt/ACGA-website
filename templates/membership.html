  • Joining the Collegiate Go Association


    The CGA is based in cooperation and networking between member clubs nationwide and is poised to make a big impact on Go in the United States. Many American Go players are unaware of nearby community and school-based clubs, and many clubs are unaware of the availability of local Go players. The Collegiate Go Association intends to correct this knowledge gap and coordinate a broader university Go culture in the United States. So why not join the growing collegiate go community? It's free!
