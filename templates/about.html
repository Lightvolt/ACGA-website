The American Collegiate Go Association was founded in 2011 to spread the magic of Go in the United States.

In July 2011, eleven students from the U.S. attended the first ever Ing Foundation China Summer Go Camp. Motivated by the culture of Go in China, and determined to spread the game, these students founded the ACGA to network between existing collegiate clubs, foster the rapid growth of Go in the United States, and contribute to the worldwide Go community.


Current goals of the ACGA include:


    • Consolidating contact information, tournament availability, resources, and needs for collegiate go clubs
    • Organize simul matches against Chinese professional players for member clubs
    • Host regional and national collegiate tournaments early in 2012
    • Provide references, resources, and organizational support for new clubs nationwide
    • Bring a delegation of Chinese college students to the United States in summer 2012 for cultural exchange and go matches

      • As a growing organization focusing on new player recruitment, we welcome your input and suggestions on how to benefit the collegiate go community. Let us know what you think!
