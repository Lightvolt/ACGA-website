Ing Chang‐Ki was a Taiwan-based industrialist and GO player. 1983 he founded the Ing Chang‐Ki Wei‐Chi Education Foundation in Taiwan with his personal wealth, and in 1996, he donated 16 million RMB to create a branch of the Foundation in Shanghai to spread the intriguing and challenging game of Go throughout the world. The Shanghai Ing Chang-Ki Wei-Chi Education Foundation is one of Mr. Ing's major philanthropic legacies, and the ACGA's major sponsor, and for its support we are extremely grateful!

For more information about the promotional and philanthropic work of the Ing Foundation, or to contact Ing representatives in the U.S. or abroad, visit the U.S. Ing Chang-Ki Wei-Chi Education Foundation website.
